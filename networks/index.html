How this page is built

Company pages are precomputed from PatentsView data. Citation views use granted patents only. Inventor networks and filing timelines use granted and pregrant records after PatentsView crosswalk deduplication. Primary CPC uses the first CPC4 in sequence; All CPC spreads citation weight across all CPC4 pairs.
