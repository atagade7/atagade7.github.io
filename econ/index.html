Methodology

Data comes from OpenAlex paper-author rows. Node radius and height track papers in the selected view, color tracks unique collaborators, and edge weight tracks repeated coauthorship. Degree, closeness, and component-scaled eigenvector centrality are computed on the full graph for each journal or for the combined five-journal graph; betweenness is computed on the full graph and approximated only when the graph is very large. The build keeps only OpenAlex records marked as journal articles and excludes obvious administration, comment, reply, and review-style pieces from the counts. Re-run scripts/build_econ_network_details.py to refresh the artifact.
